Our services

Refrigerator repair and service

When your refrigerator stops cooling, starts leaking, makes unusual noises, or the ice maker suddenly quits working, fast professional service matters. Our company provides reliable refrigerator repair and maintenance services for homeowners across San Diego and nearby areas. We work with all LG refrigerators and specialize in diagnosing both common and complex cooling system problems.

Our technicians repair French door refrigerators, side by side models, bottom freezer units, built in refrigerator systems, wine coolers, and modern smart appliances. Whether your refrigerator is running constantly, not keeping temperature, freezing food, or showing error codes, we provide accurate diagnostics and dependable repairs using quality replacement parts.

We specialize in compressor issues, evaporator fan failures, thermostat problems, sealed system repairs, water leaks, ice maker malfunctions, control board failures, and temperature inconsistencies. Many modern refrigerators, especially LG models with linear compressors, require experienced troubleshooting and proper repair procedures to prevent recurring problems.

We understand how important a working refrigerator is for your home and family. That is why we focus on fast response times, honest recommendations, and professional service designed to restore your LG refrigerator as quickly as possible. Our goal is to provide long lasting refrigerator repair solutions with clear communication and dependable customer support throughout the entire process.
